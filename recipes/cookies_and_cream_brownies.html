Cookies and Cream Brownies

Description

These brownies have both cookies and cream, making them cookies and cream brownies. Crazy, I know.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups white sugar
  • ¾ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup high-quality unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon baking powder
  • ¾ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 32 chocolate sandwich cookies (such as Oreo®), divided
  • 1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping (such as Cool Whip®), thawed

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Grease a 9-inch square baking pan.
  2. Mix dry ingretients in a mixing bowl.
  3. Whisk wet ingredients in a separate bowl. Add together and mix until combined.
  4. Pour half the batter into the pan. Smooth. Layer with oreos. Spread the remaining batter.
  5. Bake in the oven until edges are brown and center is set, 30-35 minutes.
  6. Place pan on wire rack. Let brownies cool for at least 30 minutes.
  7. Crush remaining oreos, fold into bowl with whipped topping. Spread over the top of whipped cream mixture.
